//SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Circuit Design

The Smart Plant Box is powered by an ESP32s microcontroller — the brain of the entire system. It coordinates all sensors and actuators through carefully designed GPIO connections.

Environmental Sensors

Soil moisture sensor, temperature sensor, and potentiometer for real-time monitoring and threshold adjustments.

ESP32-CAM Module

Captures daily plant photos for growth tracking and AI-powered health analysis via UART communication.

Automated Watering

Water pump motor controlled via GPIO23, activated automatically when soil moisture drops below threshold.

UV Growth Light

MOSFET-controlled grow light providing optimal light spectrum for photosynthesis and plant growth.
